Guru Nanak Dev Ji

The founder of Sikhism

Here are some interesting facts about Guru Nanak Ji:

Sri Guru Nanak Dev ji was born in 1469 in Talwandi, a village in the Sheikhupura district, 65 kms. west of Lahore.

His father was the local patwari (accountant) for crop revenue in the village of Talwandi.

He was the founder of Sikhism and the first of the ten Sikh Gurus.

On 24 September 1487 Nanak married Mata Sulakkhani in the town of Batala. The couple had two sons, Sri Chand and Lakhmi Chand.

Guru Nanak travelled extensively during his lifetime. He travelled most of South Asia and Arabia starting in 1496, at age 27, when he left his family for a thirty-year period.

A famous quote:

"Speak only that which will bring you honor"
